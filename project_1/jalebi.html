Flaky, Buttery Croissants

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 60g sugar
  • 10g salt
  • 10g instant yeast
  • 300ml warm milk
  • 300g cold butter (for laminating)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and warm milk into a dough.
  2. Roll the dough into a rectangle, place cold butter in the center, fold and seal.
  3. Roll and fold the dough 3 times, chilling between folds.
  4. Cut triangles, roll into croissants, and proof until doubled in size.
  5. Brush with egg wash and bake at 200°C (392°F) for 15-20 minutes until golden.
