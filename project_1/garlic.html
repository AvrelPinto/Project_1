Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette or loaf of bread
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, minced (or 1–2 tsp garlic paste)
  • 3 tbsp butter (softened)
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Grated cheese (optional, for cheesy garlic bread)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Slice the bread lengthwise or into thick pieces.
  3. In a small bowl, mix butter, garlic, parsley, and salt.
  4. Spread the mixture evenly on the bread slices.
  5. (Optional) Sprinkle cheese on top for extra flavor.
  6. Place the bread on a baking tray and bake for 8–10 minutes until crispy and golden.
  7. Serves Hot.
